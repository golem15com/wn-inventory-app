## Payment & Commerce System

### Business Capabilities

The platform includes a complete payment and order management system designed for real-world commerce. It handles everything from simple one-time payments to complex multi-item orders with international shipping.

#### Payment Processing

**Multi-Gateway Support**
- Integration with major payment providers (Stripe, and extensible to others)
- Cash payment workflows for delivery or in-person transactions
- Manual bank transfer instructions
- Easy addition of new payment gateways

**Payment Lifecycle Management**
- Complete payment state tracking (created, sent, accepted, rejected, refunded)
- Automatic retry logic and error handling
- Payment confirmation workflows
- Transaction history and audit trails

**International Commerce**
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Accept payments in customers' local currencies
- **Automatic Conversion**: Real-time exchange rates updated every 10 minutes
- **Currency Display**: Show prices in multiple currencies
- **Financial Precision**: No rounding errors with proper decimal handling

#### Order Management

**Complete Order Lifecycle**

The system manages orders through their entire lifecycle:

1. **Quote Phase**: Create estimates and proposals for custom work
2. **Placed Orders**: Customers create orders
3. **Payment Processing**: Awaiting and receiving payments
4. **Fulfillment**: Underway and shipped states
5. **Completion**: Finished orders
6. **Refunds**: Multiple refund scenarios (direct, deferred, with returns)
7. **Cancellations**: Handle cancelled or rejected orders

**Quote-to-Order Workflow**
- Create custom quotes for services or products
- Present quotes to customers for approval
- Convert approved quotes to actual orders
- Perfect for service businesses, custom manufacturing, or consulting

#### Shopping Cart & Checkout

**Flexible Cart System**
- Multi-item carts with quantity management
- Price calculations (net, gross, taxes)
- Shipping cost integration
- Discount and adjustment support
- Guest checkout or authenticated user checkout
- Cart persistence and recovery

**Shipping Management**
- Multiple shipping methods with individual pricing
- Tax calculation on shipping
- Currency-aware shipping costs
- Shipping status tracking

#### Guest Checkout

- No account required to place orders
- Optional registration after purchase
- Guest order tracking with unique codes
- Convert guests to registered users for repeat purchases

#### Automated Communications

**Order & Payment Notifications**
- 20+ email templates for every order and payment state
- Automatic customer notifications (order received, payment confirmed, shipped, etc.)
- Internal employee notifications
- Customizable email templates
- Multi-language email support
